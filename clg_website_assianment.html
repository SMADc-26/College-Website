Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1986 and affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology is a premier engineering institution located in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Named after the legendary engineer and Bharat Ratna awardee Sir M. Visvesvaraya, our institute embodies his vision of technical excellence and nation-building through education.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong industry connect, SMVIT has consistently produced engineers who excel in their fields and contribute meaningfully to society.
